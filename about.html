Our Story

Fresh Produce, Trusted by Gandhidham

Vinayak Fruit & Vegetable Mart began with a simple goal — to bring the freshest fruits and vegetables to every home in Gandhidham at honest, transparent prices. Located in Maheshwari Nagar, we source produce directly from farmers each morning so quality is never compromised.

From premium Kesar mangoes in season to everyday essentials like potatoes, onions and tomatoes, we make daily grocery shopping convenient, affordable and reliable. Order easily over WhatsApp and enjoy fast home delivery.
